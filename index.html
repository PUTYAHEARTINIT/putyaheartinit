The Book

Your Heart Isn't Just an Organ —
It's an Operating System.

"When you learn to master your heart, you learn to master your life."

In PUT YA HEART IN IT, Stevie Harts reveals the real power behind every creation: the energy field of the heart. Blending spirituality, science, and street sense, this book breaks down how your emotions shape reality — and how to align with the universal rhythm that governs it all.

17 chapters. Practices in every one. Written like a big brother talking to you on the couch. This isn't theory — it's a guide to feeling your way into purpose, healing, and divine alignment.
